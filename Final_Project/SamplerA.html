Sampler A

Sample A consists of audio samples, microphone input and effect processing. To begin with, please press the "On" button to trigger the Sampler. Bubble, Drone1 and Drone2 are audio files. Mic input is the microphone input. Both audio files and microphone input could be processed by audio effect processing. Users could choose to move to sliders to change the level of audio effects.
